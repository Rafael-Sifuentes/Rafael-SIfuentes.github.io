Some frequently asked
questions

How much will it cost ?

The cost of tinting can very between each vehicle, but generally you should expect to pay a few hundred dollars for a quality tint job. It is always best to call and ask for a quote for your specific vehicle to get the most accurate pricing.

State of Texas window tint laws

The Texas tint law for the sides of a non commercial vehicle are simple. The front two doors must have a net ( tint film plus glass ) visible light transmission of at least 25%. There are no darkness restrictions on windows located behind the front two doors, as long as the vehicle is equipped with side view mirrors.

How long will it take ?

A tinting job takes anywhere between 45 minutes and several hours, depending on the number of windows you want to be tinted. If the tint is only applied to two front side windows, then expect the job to take much shorter than if all side windows and the back window were being tinted. Sometimes, we will ask for a day to do the best job and allow enough time for the film to dry. Generally, it's advisable to make arrangements for a ride on the day you intend to take your car for tinting.

What are the benifits of tinting ?

The many benefits of window film include eye comfort, UV protection, aesthetics, and increased safety, privacy, and energy savings.

What types of window tint do you offer ?

We offer a range of window tint including regular and ceramic. Both of which come in 70%, 50%, 35%, 20%, and 5% opacity.

What should I use to clean my windows after I've had them tinted?

After window film installation, wait a week before cleaning the inside of the window where the film was installed. After a week, use a window cleaner without ammonia and a soft cloth or rubber squeegee. We recommend foam cleaners because they do not streak, and are usually ammonia-free. Never scrape the film with any type of blade or clean your tinted windows with anything abrasive, as it will scratch the film.
